Project 03

Logos

As part of my Project 1 me and a partner came up with a company name called Fresh Pixels, I decided to start designing a logo and this is what I came up with. I was asked to design a new logo for a multicultural enterprising scheme that would incorporate the four countries flags together, I decided to warp them together so that it would look somewhat globe shape to represent that the 4 countries are able to come together despite cultural or ethnicity differences.
